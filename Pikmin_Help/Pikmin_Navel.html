The Forest Navel (unlocked after 5 parts are retrieved)

The Forest Navel is the third area in the first Pikmin game. The entire area is underground and is accessed by a hole in a vast clearing of the forest. According to Captain Olimar, from high altitude it looks like a belly button, hence its name. One of the distinguishing features of this cave is the neighboring lakes of water at different altitudes, which are based on the real world structures called rimstone dams. This area is where Olimar meets Blue Pikmin for the first time. Nine pieces of his ship, the Dolphin, can be found in the area, as well as many new enemies. It is the only Pikmin area not to be remade in Pikmin 2, but remade in Pikmin 3 as downloadable content – it is known as Forgotten Cove.

Bosses/Mini-Bosses

  • Breadbug - Part held: Space Float

    • “This pesky scavenger is truly annoying. It attempts to steal the spoils that the Pikmin earn in battle. If the Pikmin chase it too far, they can be dragged into its lair along with their food. It may look weak, but its appearance is deceiving.” - Captain Olimar

  • Puffstool - Part held: Omega Stabilizer

    • “This creature's cap is elastic, negating any Pikmin attacks. Attacking its lower torso works better, but the puffstool lets out spores that turn the Pikmin into mushrooms, which then attack me! They return to normal when shaken off.” - Captain Olimar

  • Beady Long Legs - Part held: Guard Satellite

    • “This creature is so large, it would tower over the Dolphin. All the Pikmin can do is cling to its feet and try to slow it down. Its large round torso is its sole weak point - since Yellow Pikmin fly highest, they should be most effective against it.” - Captain Olimar

Parts

  • #1 Ionium Jet - min: 15 blue Pikmin

    • "I've found my #1 Ionium Jet! Unfortunately, this puts out a slightly odoriferous exhaust, but it does propel me to escape velocity in an instant. The stench is a small price to pay for such performance!" - Captain Olimar

  • Gravity Jumper - min: 25 blue Pikmin

    • "I've found my Gravity Jumper! By manipulating the forces of gravity, this key component gives me the final boost I need to make the jump to super light speed. Good thing I found it." - Captain Olimar

  • Automatic Gear - min: 15 any color Pikmin

    • "I've found the Automatic Gear! This thing chugs right along at its own pace. It makes piloting much easier, giving me time to concentrate on the finer points of space travel." - Captain Olimar

  • Space Float - min: 30 any color Pikmin

    • "This float is an absolute necessity for any pilot who lacks skill at swimming in space. An excellent swimmer like me has no need for something like this, but my motto is 'always be prepared.' Really, it's just for emergencies." - Captain Olimar

  • Anti-Dioxin Filter - min: 40 blue Pikmin

    • "It's the Anti-Dioxin Filter! This fits over the rocket's exhaust port and filters out all disease-causing agents from the ship's exhaust. That means I'll be able to move around without polluting the planet's atmosphere! I feel worlds better!" - Captain Olimar

  • Libra - min: 15 red Pikmin

    • "I've found my Libra! My daughter gave this to me. It's named after her astrological sign. My sweet little girl... I wonder what she's doing right at this moment..." - Captain Olimar

  • Analog Computer - min: 20 red Pikmin

    • "I've found my Analog Computer! This computer conveys the kind of vague data that falls outside the range of ones and zeros. Actually, to be honest, it's a little bit too vague, so it isn't very helpful." - Captain Olimar

  • Omega Stabilizer - min: 30 red Pikmin

    • "I've found the Omega Stabilizer! I absolutely must have this piece if my battered ship is ever to fly straight again. It has steered me through countless deep space storms. That, and it looks rather cool." - Captain Olimar

  • Guard Satellite - min: 20 red Pikmin

    • "The Guard Satellite! Deep space is filled with dangers. This automated satellite does its part to help guard both me and my spaceship. I'll sleep better at night once this little satellite is back on duty." - Captain Olimar
