The Impact Site (Starting Area)

The Impact Site is where Captain Olimar first lands after his ship crashes on PNF-404 in Pikmin, and so the first day is spent there. The area is near the edge of the vast forest on the area select map, not far from The Forest of Hope. The Impact Site is the location of the discovery of the Red Onion with its associated Red Pikmin. This area and The Final Trial make up the Wistful Wild in Pikmin 2. Because of its size and few ship parts, this area is one of the least visited in a regular playthrough, but is also the best one to increase Pikmin population in.
